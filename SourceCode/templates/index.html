About Sahay

Sahay is a comprehensive disaster management and response hub designed to equip individuals and communities with the resources and knowledge needed to navigate through emergencies effectively. With features like Dos and Don'ts, providing essential guidelines for various disaster scenarios, and a Rainfall Analysis page that offers insights into past rainfall trends, Sahay empowers users to stay prepared. The app also includes an Emergency Contacts page to ensure quick access to critical support during crises and a Blood Donation Information section to guide users on compatible blood types, aiding life-saving decisions. With more features in development, Sahay aims to be a one-stop solution for disaster preparedness and response, fostering resilience and safety for all.
