Standard Operating Procedure

Earthquake

Do's

  • Drop, cover, and hold on.
  • Stay indoors until the shaking stops.
  • Provide first aid if necessary.

Don'ts

  • Do not use elevators.
  • Avoid standing near windows.
  • Do not re-enter damaged buildings.

Flood

Do's

  • Move to higher ground.
  • Follow evacuation orders.
  • Turn off utilities if needed.

Don'ts

  • Do not walk through flood water.
  • Avoid driving in flooded areas.
  • Do not touch electrical appliances if wet.

Cyclone

Do's

  • Stay indoors and away from windows.
  • Secure outdoor objects.
  • Listen to updates.

Don'ts

  • Do not go outside during the storm.
  • Avoid using electrical appliances.
  • Do not drive unnecessarily.

Landslide

Do's

  • Move away from landslide path.
  • Stay alert for unusual sounds.
  • Follow local warnings.

Don'ts

  • Do not return until declared safe.
  • Avoid steep slopes.
  • Do not ignore alerts.
