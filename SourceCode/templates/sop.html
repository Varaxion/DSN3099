Your guide to effective disaster management and response

Earthquake

Do's

  • Drop, cover, and hold on during the shaking.
  • Stay indoors until the shaking stops.
  • Check for injuries and provide first aid if necessary.

Don'ts

  • Do not use elevators.
  • Avoid standing near windows or heavy furniture.
  • Do not re-enter damaged buildings.

Flood

Do's

  • Move to higher ground immediately.
  • Follow evacuation orders from authorities.
  • Turn off utilities at the main switches if instructed.

Don'ts

  • Do not walk through moving water.
  • Avoid driving in flooded areas.
  • Do not touch electrical equipment if wet.

Cyclone

Do's

  • Stay indoors and away from windows.
  • Secure outdoor objects that could blow away.
  • Listen to the radio for updates.

Don'ts

  • Do not go outside during the storm.
  • Avoid using electrical appliances.
  • Do not drive unless absolutely necessary.

Landslide

Do's

  • Move away from the path of the landslide.
  • Listen for unusual sounds that might indicate moving debris.
  • Stay alert if you are in areas prone to landslides.

Don'ts

  • Do not return to the area until it is declared safe.
  • Avoid building near steep slopes or mountain edges.
  • Do not ignore landslide warnings.
