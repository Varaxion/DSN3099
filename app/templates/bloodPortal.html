Compatibility Guidelines

Transfusion compatibility depends on matching A, B, and Rh blood factor antigens. Safe transfusions protect the receiver's immune response:

Blood Group Can Receive From
Type A A, O
Type B B, O
Type AB (Universal Receiver) AB, A, B, O
Type O (Universal Red Donor) O (O- to anyone)

* Type AB is considered the universal plasma donor, as plasma from AB-type donors can be transfused to recipients with A, B, AB, or O blood types.
